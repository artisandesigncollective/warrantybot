# WarrantyBot — Business Plan & PRD

## Market
- **TAM:** $50B+ in annual global warranty-eligible repairs.
- **SAM:** 50M US households with major appliances/late-model cars.
- **SOM Year 1:** 4,800 users = $187,200 ARR.

## Revenue: $39 Flat Fee
- Unit Economics: Revenue $39, Stripe $1.43, LLM $0.30, **Margin: $37.27 (95%)**.

## PRD — Feature Matrix
### ✅ Built
- Landing Page & $39 Stripe Checkout.
- Magnuson-Moss Demand RAG Generator (Demand Letter + FTC Complaint).

### ⬜ Planned
- Database of manufacturer warranty terms.
- Automated "Registered Agent" address lookup.
- Escalation to State Attorney General.

## Roadmap
- **Phase 1 ✅:** MVP.
- **Phase 2:** Database integration for "secret" manufacturer recalls.
- **Phase 3:** Automated certified mail sending.
